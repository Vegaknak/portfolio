About me

My name is Anna, I am a HCI Master student (Human-Computer Interaction). With a background in psychology and a great enthusiasm towards technology, I hope to connect the two through human-centered design. As an advocate and enthusiast of science, I firmly believe that most decision-making processes can be significantly improved when grounded in data.

My friends describe me as artistic, structured, hands-on, determined and ambitious. I have more interests and hobbies than the twenty four hours in the day permit me to have; I play bass guitar in a band and occasionally spin records at house parties. I love to dance, to read, to cook and to be out in nature. Though I am ambitious and hard-working, a healthy work-life balance is important to me and I value my relationships and personal development highly.
